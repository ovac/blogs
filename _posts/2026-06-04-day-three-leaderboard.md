Quick mid-contest note.

Day three of [Round 2](https://gidipex.com/contests). The Top 5 on the Vibe Trading Challenge leaderboard doesn't overlap with the Round 1 Top 5. Different traders, different markets, different play styles.

That's the signal I was hoping for. If the same five traders won every round, the contest would be gameable. It isn't. The mechanic is sorting traders by skill and risk appetite, not by who already knew about us.

Spot traders cluster mid-pack. Futures traders appear at the top AND the bottom of the leaderboard. The contest is doing what a healthy book should do, letting risk-taking get rewarded when it works and punished when it doesn't.

Three weeks left. Free entry still open.
